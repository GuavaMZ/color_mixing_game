# Color Lab - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 27, 2026

## Introduction

Color Lab ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile game.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect Directly

Color Lab is designed with privacy in mind. We **DO NOT** directly:
- Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Track your precise location
- Access your contacts or photos
- Require account creation or login

### Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored **only on your device** using local storage:
- Game progress (levels completed, stars earned)
- Settings preferences (sound, language, accessibility options)
- Unlocked achievements and cosmetic items
- Tutorial completion status
- High scores and statistics
- In-game currency balances and lab upgrades

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not backed up to our servers
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app
- Cannot be recovered if you lose your device

## Third-Party Services

While Color Lab does not directly collect personal data, we integrate third-party services that may collect information strictly to provide their respective services:

### Advertising
We use **Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data (such as device identifiers and general location data) to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information on how Google uses data, please refer to [Google's Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

### In-App Purchases
We use standard app store billing services (such as **Google Play Billing** and **Apple App Store**) to process in-app purchases. When you make a purchase, the transaction is processed directly by the respective app store. We do not collect, process, or store your payment information (e.g., credit card details).

## Permissions

Color Lab requests minimal permissions:
- **Internet**: Required to serve advertisements and process in-app purchases. Core gameplay works fully offline.
- **Vibration**: For haptic feedback during gameplay (can be disabled in settings).

## Children's Privacy

Color Lab is a puzzle game suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to our third-party partners without your consent, please contact the respective third-party service or reach out to us.

## Data Security

Since your game data is stored locally on your device, your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:
- Using device encryption
- Setting a secure lock screen
- Keeping your device's operating system updated

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or services. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
