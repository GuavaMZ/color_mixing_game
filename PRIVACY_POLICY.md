# Color Lab - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

## Introduction

Color Lab ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile game, especially regarding our cloud synchronization and security features.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect Directly

Color Lab is designed with privacy in mind. We **DO NOT** directly:
- Collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your real name, email, or phone number.
- Track your precise GPS location.
- Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files on your device.

### Cloud Synchronization (Firebase)

To provide a consistent experience across devices and prevent data loss, we use **Firebase** (a Google service) for cloud synchronization:
- **Anonymous Authentication**: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create a unique, non-identifiable ID for your game session. This ID is not linked to your real-world identity.
- **Game Progress & Data**: Your game progress (levels, stars, coins, achievements) is synchronized to **Firebase Firestore**. This data is encrypted and stored securely to allow you to restore your progress if you change devices or reinstall the app.
- **Security Events**: We log certain security-related events (such as detected tampering or impossible game states) to our internal audit log. This information is used strictly for maintaining the integrity of the game and is not used to identify you.

### Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored on your device and synchronized with our secure cloud storage:
- Game progress (levels completed, stars earned)
- Settings preferences (sound, language, accessibility options)
- Unlocked achievements and cosmetic items
- In-game currency balances and lab upgrades
- Statistics and high scores

## Third-Party Services

While Color Lab does not directly collect personal data, we integrate third-party services that may collect information strictly to provide their respective services:

### Firebase (Google)
We use Firebase for cloud storage, authentication, and core infrastructure. Firebase may collect certain device identifiers and usage data to ensure service reliability. For more information, please see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Advertising
We use **Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data (such as advertising IDs and general location data) to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. You can manage your ad preferences in your device settings.

### In-App Purchases
We use standard app store billing services (**Google Play Billing** and **Apple App Store**) for transactions. We do not collect or store your payment details; these are handled entirely by the respective app stores.

### Cloud Messaging & Notifications
We use **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)** to send important game updates, news, and alerts.
- **FCM Tokens**: To route notifications to your device, we use a unique FCM token. These tokens are handled by Firebase and are not used to identify your real-world identity.
- **Local Notifications**: We use local notifications for offline game alerts (e.g., reminding you of life restoration). These are processed entirely on your device.
- **Opt-out**: You can enable or disable notifications at any time through your device's system settings.

## Permissions

Color Lab requests minimal permissions:
- **Internet**: Required for cloud synchronization, advertisements, and in-app purchases.
- **Vibration**: For haptic feedback during gameplay.

## Children's Privacy

Color Lab is a puzzle game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our cloud sync uses anonymous identifiers that do not constitute personal information under applicable regulations.

## Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your data, including:
- **Encryption**: Sensitive game data is encrypted using dynamic keys before being stored or transmitted.
- **Integrity Checks**: We use HMAC and sequence numbers to prevent data tampering.
- **Secure Storage**: On-device data is stored using secure storage mechanisms where available.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our features or legal requirements. Updates will be indicated by the "Last Updated" date.
